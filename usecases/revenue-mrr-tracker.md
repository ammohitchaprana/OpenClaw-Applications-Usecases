# Revenue & MRR Tracker

Fetch Stripe revenue data daily, calculate MRR/ARR/churn, and send a morning summary with trend indicators.

## Skills You Need

- Stripe
- Slack/Telegram
- Cron

After installing the required skills, prompt your OpenClaw with your specific configuration for this use case.
