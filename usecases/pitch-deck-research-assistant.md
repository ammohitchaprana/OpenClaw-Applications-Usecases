# Pitch Deck Research Assistant

Given a market thesis, research TAM/SAM/SOM, find comparable funding rounds, and compile into a structured brief for your pitch deck.

## Skills You Need

- Web search
- Browser
- Google Sheets

After installing the required skills, prompt your OpenClaw with your specific configuration for this use case.
